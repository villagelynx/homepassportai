How HomePassportAI helps

Photos and serials, ready when you need them.

OpenAI API key (required for AI)

HomePassportAI uses your OpenAI key on this device. You pay OpenAI directly for photo and video analysis — the site does not include shared AI credits.

  • Needed for AI: label/model/serial extraction, appliance photos, room video scans.
  • Works without AI: saving photos, editing details, search, share, manual lookup links, insurance PDF (from saved items).

Why use it

  • Know what you own — brands, models, and serial numbers in one place.
  • Insurance ready — download a PDF inventory for claims or renewals.
  • Repairs faster — look up manuals and save your go-to repair companies per item.
  • Cloud backup — sign in to sync across devices (optional).
  • Share an item — send details to a spouse, landlord, or technician from your phone.

Paintings & artwork

Photograph the whole piece with corners visible. AI can detect dominant colors, guess approximate size, read corner signatures, and build a combined signature image where the label photo normally goes — artist in Brand, title in Model.

Add an appliance (quick scan)

  1. Tap the camera button on the home screen.
  2. Take a photo of the appliance.
  3. Optionally add a label photo for model and serial (or skip it).
  4. Optionally add a receipt photo.
  5. Review the details, pick a room, and save.

Scan a whole room

Record a short room video — the app finds multiple items and lets you choose which ones to keep.

Find items later

  • Use search on the home list (nickname, brand, model, serial).
  • Tap a room chip to filter by room (toggle in Settings).
  • Open any item for photos, edit details, manuals, and repair notes.

Settings worth knowing

  • OpenAI API key — required on your device for AI label and room video analysis.
  • Sign in — sync inventory to the cloud.
  • Add to iPhone home screen — open like an app.
  • Insurance PDF and backup — export or restore your inventory.

Settings

AI analysis uses your API key on this device — OpenAI or Claude. The app never uses a shared developer key.

AI provider (required for AI)

Choose OpenAI or Claude, then paste your key. It stays on this device and is sent only when you run AI analysis. You pay the provider directly — HomePassportAI does not bill for AI. Without a key you can still save items and documents manually.
